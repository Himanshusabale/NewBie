Hostel And Mess

"Hostels are residential facilities provided by universities or colleges to accommodate students, typically situated on or near campus grounds. They offer various types of rooms. Alongside hostels, institutions provide mess facilities, offering dining options for students, faculty, and staff. These facilities serve a variety of meals, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. Some institutions offer meal plans for students to purchase, providing a set number of meals per week or semester. Costs for hostel accommodation and meals vary depending on factors like room type, meal plan chosen, and additional services offered."
